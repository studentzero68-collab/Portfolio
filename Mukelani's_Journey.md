## Case Study — Netflix Landing Page

**What was the problem?**
I wanted to push my HTML and CSS skills further and build something that looked and felt like a real product.

**What did I build?**
A responsive Netflix-inspired landing page with a modern dark UI, hero section and feature highlights.

**What did I learn?**
I learned a lot about layout, spacing and how to make a page feel polished. I also learned how to fix image issues without just asking AI for the answer — I worked through it myself.

**What would I do differently?**
I would add more interactivity — a working carousel or a sign up form with validation.

**What am I proud of?**
This is my most independently built project. The image corrections and layout fixes — I did those myself. For someone who is still learning that means a lot.
