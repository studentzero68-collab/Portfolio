About Me

I am Mukelani N. Sindana, a 20-year-old Full Stack Web Developer in training studying at iHub Africa, based in Gauteng, South Africa.

I didn't come from a background that handed me everything so I learned early that if I want something I have to go and get it myself. Web development is that thing for me right now.

I started coding because I needed something that was mine. Something I could build a future on — not just for me but for the family I want to have one day.

What drives me is building things that actually matter — charity platforms, community pages and experiences that make people feel something.

I am quick to adapt, I don't mind making mistakes and I don't pretend to know everything. I just keep going.
